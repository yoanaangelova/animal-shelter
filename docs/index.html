Who are we

Second Chance Animal Shelter has been operating since 2010. it has grown within 3 cities and 5 buildings. It started ofas a small local shelter for abondaned animals and soon began to shelter animals of all shapes and sizes. Nowadays there are more than 30 rescuers and 15 volunteers who help animals.
